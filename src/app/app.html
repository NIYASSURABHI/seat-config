AAE's CrewRider is a lightweight, modular seating solution for specialist vehicle interiors. Available in jump-seat and driver/commander variants, it offers compact folding options and adaptable mounting to support efficient crew layouts. CrewRider prioritizes flexibility and space optimization for everyday mission profiles.

The BlastRider Driver seat integrates a full Blast Mitigation System (BMS) housed within a purpose-built structural frame. It can be configured with multiple comfort and safety features, including a headrest, whiplash bar, and armrests tailored for long-duration missions. This variant delivers high protection while supporting the driver's ergonomic and operational needs.

AAE's CrewRider Driver/Commander is a modular seating system designed for operator positions in specialist vehicles.
